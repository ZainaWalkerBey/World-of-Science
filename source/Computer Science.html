What is Computer Science?


Computer science is a type of science that studies the basis of computers.

Origin

The origin of computer science can be traced to two unique men.
These two men were known as Thomas J. Watson Sr.and Benjamin Wood.
In 1928, Wood invited Benjamin to a meeting and explained how
machines can be used to do mathematics and analysis for a variety of
fields. These machines were known as IBM100. This concept intrigued
Watson, which lead him to donate a few of the IBM100 machines. Watson
later became a trustee at the University of Columbia, the school in which
Benjamin worked at. This allowed computer science to become a course at
the school, due to the IBM100 machines.
